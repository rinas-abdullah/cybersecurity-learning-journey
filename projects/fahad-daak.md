# 🏗 Fahad Daak – Engineering & Design Platform

## 📌 Overview
Fahad Daak Engineering Consultants is a professional engineering and architectural firm based in Jeddah, Saudi Arabia, specializing in modern architectural design and consultancy services. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}  

This project highlights the digital presence and system thinking behind managing engineering services, client interactions, and project workflows.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Engineering firms often face challenges such as:

- Managing multiple client requests  
- Difficulty organizing project workflows  
- Lack of centralized digital systems  
- Inefficient communication with clients

## 💡 Solution
A structured digital approach for engineering services including:

|-----------------|------------|
| 🌐 Website       | Showcases services, projects, and contact channels |
| 🧾 Client Flow   | Organizes customer requests and project tracking |
| 📊 Management    | Supports structured workflow for engineering projects |
