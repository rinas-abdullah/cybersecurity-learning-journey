# 🚗 Beirut Al-Raed – Automotive Service Center System

## 📌 Overview
Beirut Al-Raed is an automotive service center providing vehicle maintenance and repair services across multiple branches in Saudi Arabia.

This project presents a system-oriented view of managing automotive services, customer flow, and operational efficiency through digital platforms.

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Automotive service centers often face challenges such as:

- Managing high volumes of customer requests  
- Lack of organized booking systems  
- Difficulty tracking service operations  
- Inefficient communication with customers  

---

## 💡 Solution
A structured service management system that includes:

| Component        | Description |
|-----------------|------------|
| 🌐 Website       | Displays services, branches, and contact information |
| 📅 Booking Flow  | Organizes service requests and appointments |
| 🧾 Service System| Tracks vehicle services and maintenance workflow |
| 📊 Management    | Improves operational efficiency and tracking |

---

## 🚗 Service Features
- Vehicle maintenance and repair services  
- Multiple branch management (Jeddah / Riyadh)  
- Customer communication channels (WhatsApp / social media)
